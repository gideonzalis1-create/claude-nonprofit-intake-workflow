# Human Review Checklist

- Did Claude stay within the note, or did it infer facts not provided?
- Did Claude avoid promising program eligibility or benefits outcomes?
- Is the output respectful, plain-language, and non-judgmental?
- Has real personally identifiable information been removed before use?
- Are urgent safety, medical, legal, or housing issues routed to qualified staff or emergency protocols?
- Could another staff member understand the next step without re-reading the full intake note?
- Did staff record any AI mistake so the workflow can improve?
